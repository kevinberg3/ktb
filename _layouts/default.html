Who is this guy?




Hey I'm Kevin - seeing as you're here, I presume you'll want to know more about me. I grew up in Toronto, Canada and attended the University of Toronto graduating with some knowledge in finance, accounting and economics, amongst other things.

Upon graduating, I took a trip around the world. It was pretty cool.

After that, I started working at PwC (big accounting firm), where I've spent the past couple of years gaining experience in the the financial reporting and operations of mining and tech companies. I also learned some excel and financial modelling skills while I was at it. They threw in a cool designation with letters to add to my name too.

Somewhere along the way, I became obsessed with learning and how to make new things. I ultimately stumbled upon a school called Bitmaker Labs, which was able to teach me how to become an entry level developer in 9 weeks. This site is a function of my experience there

In my free time, I enjoy playing sports, camping and learning about pretty much anything that's foreign to me. I'm a dead-eye shooting guard on the basketball court and can be found riding in a peleton on weekends.
